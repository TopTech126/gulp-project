CarMart Cookie Policy

Effective Date: 2024 Jan 25

1. Introduction

Welcome to CarMart. We use cookies on our website to enhance your browsing experience and collect information about your interactions with our site. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your options for managing cookies.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They serve various purposes, including remembering your preferences, analyzing website performance, and delivering relevant content and advertisements.

3. How We Use Cookies

3.1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly. They include session management and security cookies.

3.2. Performance Cookies

We use performance cookies to analyze how visitors use our website. This helps us improve our website's performance and user experience.

3.3. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember your preferences, such as language and region settings. They enhance your user experience by providing personalized features.

3.4. Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements to you. They track your online activities and may share information with third-party advertisers.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. You can choose to accept or reject cookies and delete cookies stored on your device. Please note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website, and some features may not work correctly.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party cookies, such as those from advertising and analytics providers. These third parties may collect information about your online activities across different websites, including our site.

6. Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our use of cookies. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
